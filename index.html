Headline about Ugo

Ugo isn't your average garbage collector. This alien scrapper cleans up the galaxy by day and dominates the underground fighting circuit by night. He's a three-time galactic champion in an illegal, intergalactic fighting league the IGFC, and he's quickly becoming a legend. The Ugo meme coin embodies this underdog spirit, capturing the humor and grit of an extraterrestrial brawler who's as comfortable with a trash compactor as he is with a knockout punch. .
